Biography


Henghui Ding is currently a Postdoctoral Researcher at Computer Vision Lab of ETH Zürich in Switzerland, working with Prof. Fisher Yu. He was a Research Scientist at ByteDance AI Lab in Singapore. He received the Ph.D. degree (winner of Best Thesis Award) from Nanyang Technological University (NTU), Singapore, advised by Prof. Xudong Jiang, and Prof. Gang Wang. Prior to that, he received the B.E. degree from Xi'an Jiaotong University (XJTU), Xi'an, China. His research interests include computer vision, language, machine learning, with special emphasis on high-level image/video recognition and pixel-level scene understanding.
If you are interested in joining/visitng (or remotely collaborating with) our group at ETH, please do not hesitate to drop me an email with your resume. We will usually reply within a week if we have a suitable position.
